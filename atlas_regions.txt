HARVARD-OXFORD HUMAN BRAIN ATLAS — Valid Region Names for generate_brain_nilearn.py
================================================================================

HOW TO USE:
  Copy any label below exactly as written into the LESION_REGIONS list.
  Hemisphere is controlled by the second field: "right", "left", or "both".
  Subcortical labels already include Left/Right — use just the name part.
  Cortical labels have no prefix — hemisphere is applied spatially.
